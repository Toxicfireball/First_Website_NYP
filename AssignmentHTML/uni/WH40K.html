Warhammer 40K

What is Warhammer 40K?

Welcome to Warhammer 40k, one of the most over the top science fiction universes. Filled with hundreds of stories. Like its counterpart, Warhammer Fantasy, Wahrammer 40k was build to be a tabletop game though overtime it has evolved into something more. Warhammer 40K is famous for the sheer amount of stories in it. To properly know every story would take ages due to the sheer amount of content there is. Warhammer 40K is known for its "grimdark" setting which means that there is truly no good faction and every is evil in some way

Premise

Warhammer 40K is set in the 40,000 years in the future. Humanity is the dominant power in the galaxy as the Imperium of man, but is an evil,genocidal,Xenophobic regime with little to no good qualities. It is threatened by multiple alien races, but most of all against the four chaos gods who have chipped away at all. Humaniy is in name led by the Emperor of mankind, a tyrant who once had a vision for humanity. Now, the vision is shattered due to his own hubris and mankind, but not mankind alone has to fight of the threat. The aliens of the Aeldari,Necrons and Tau have fought humanity, either for their survival or to assert dominace but all share a common enemy of chaos. The Warhammer 40K setting is one of never ending war, where hope is but embers in a galaxy of darkness.

What's so special about Warhammer 40K?

Warhammer 40k well liked due to its extreme dark nature. As said before, there are no good guys. Humanity is made very clear to be evil, the Imperium of man is stated to be "the cruelest regime possible". There is a constant sense of doom hanging over everyone. Warhammer 40K also boast some of the most crazy things seen in science fictiomn. War machines the shaps of catherdrals,the size of mounatins and with the firepower of nuclear weapons. While it's not releastic by any means, many do enjoy its over the top nature.
