Star Wars

Everyone's familiar with Star Wars!, if you aren't, you are missing out on a lot!. Star Wars is the most famous science fantasy out there. It boast's hundred's of books, dozen's of movies, Tv shows and books. First started in 1977 with the release of the first movie, Star Wars has grown into something of a phenomenon, being one of the most popular media franchises ever. Star wars has some of the largest fanbase around the globe. Star wars even have their own theme park!

Premise

Star Wars is set famously in "A galaxy far far away". The main focus of Star Wars is around the struggle over "The Force" a magical...well power that seeminglt binds the galaxy together. The Force is split between the light and the dark side of it with the Jedi and Sith representing the respective sides. That is not all in Star Wars as it also features as the name implies, inter-galatactic war between the Sith and Jedi influenced factions. Star wars takes place over three main time period that the nine movies revolve around. it. With the time periods revolving around the fall of the Republic, the Galactic civil war and the rise of the First Order. While opinions are divided on the quality of each, all era's have their fair share of great stories.

Canon vs Legends

There is a divided between the stories in the Star Wars universe that are split between Canon and Legend. Canon stories are stories writen after Diseny took over the franchise, while legend are stories written before. Only the prequel and orginal trilogy and the 2008 CLone Wars series are part of both catagories. Legend has far more material compared to the canon and a far deeper universe but its no longer being updated. The Canon storyline on the other hand has been getting new movies,books,Tv shows and games every year.In the end it is perfectly fine to prefer one over the other or simply like both!
