The Expanse

The Expanse is one of the most underwatched shows, especially sci-fi wise. It's an incredible show with great visuals, a lot of attention to detail as well as being extremely plausaible outside of a few quirks. The Expanse is both a book and Tv series with the book series nearing completion with the show series airing it 6th season at the end of this year. The Expanse has plenty of science, action and politics in it!

t

Premise

The Expanse takes place in our solar system in the year 2350. Humanity has colonised the solar system but it is not unnited. Humanity is split into three distinct faction: The UN which mainly occupys Earth and Luna, the MCR(Martain congressional republic)which controls Mars and is in a cold war with Earth and the OPA(Outer planet alliance) a loose alliance of people ranging from political idealist to outright terrorist who live in the asteroid belts who have been oppressed by Earth and Mars. The world of the Expanse is powder keg, waiting to explode and an alien substance causes just that.

Why so many love it

The Expanse is loved by its fanbase for being one of the most releastic science-fiction shows. There is no artifical gravity, energy shields, lasers or spacefighters. The world has plenty of intrigue and suspence build on by its grounded nature. For example, people on Earth have to wait hours to know what is happening on the other side of the galaxy. Accelerating to fast onboard a ship will cause immense stress or even kill its occupants. The fragility of everything makes the Expanse so loved by its Fans
