A Song of Ice and Fire

I think everyone has heard of Game of Thrones, one of the most famous TV shows of the generation. It is based on the book series of A Song of Ice an Fire. It is a series dripping with intrigue and mystery as different sides try to get an advantage over on another. It is an epic low Fantasy series with dozens of characters each with extremely interesting backstory and viewpoint.

Premise

The world of A Song of Ice and Fire is set in a earth like planet called planetos. Though it is mostly set in the continent of Westeros. Westeros is rulled by a King who in turn rules of seven houses each with their own vassals. These houses constantly fight for power as they attempt to increase their standing. To the north, a huge ice wall guards Westeros from the wildings that live beyond yet that might not be all that lives there. To the east, exiles from a toppled dynasty begin to remerge.

But what about Game of Thrones? Heard the last season was bad

Game of thrones is the TV adapation of the ASOIAF books, it became immensly succesfull quickly. However, around the 5th season, the show started to deviate away from the book, cutting down and ruining plotlines. This became clear in the later seasons as the show's storytelling plummeted drastically. That is why the show recieved so much backlash on its 8th season.
