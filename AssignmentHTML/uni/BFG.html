Battlestar Galactica

Battlestar Galactica is one of the more "Classic" science ficton TV shows. It follows a war between humanity and machines known as cylons and the relationship between both sides. It features as reminace pf humanity are confined to only a single fleet of ships as they are chased by the Cylons while they try to find Earth, their "true" home. The series is well known for being one of the few sci-fi shows to break out of the sci-fi "niche".
