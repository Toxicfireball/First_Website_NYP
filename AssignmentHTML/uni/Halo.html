Halo

Halo is a universe that is primarily a first person shooter but also have a wide variety of other media such as book. Famous for its First person shooter games, Halo is one of the most recorgnised science-fiction universe. It is set 500 years in the future where humanity is waging a war against an alien alliance hell bend on genociding mankind. It's premise is simple to get into.

Premise

The original trilogy centers on an interstellar war between humanity and an alliance of aliens known as The Covenant. The Covenant, led by their religious leaders called the Prophets, worship an ancient civilization known as the Forerunners, who perished while defeating the parasitic Flood. The eponymous Halo Array are a group of immense, habitable, ring-shaped superweapons that were created by the Forerunners to destroy the Flood, but which the Covenant mistake for religious artifacts that, if activated, would transport them on a Great Journey to meet the Forerunners. Halo is famous for its Spartans, genetically modified super soilders that were created initially to crush human rebellion. Why so many love it

Halo is famous for being a force for influence the First person shooter genre in many ways. Having left a major mark on the genre with the original genre. The premise is simple but has many layers of depths. For example, Spartans, one of the iconic figures of the series, are actually kidnapped teenages brainwashed to become super soilders for the goverment in order to crush rebellion. Yet they later became the beacon of hope for mankind, which leads to the question if such a decision was right.
