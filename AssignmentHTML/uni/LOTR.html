Lord of the Rings

What is Lord of the Rings?

Ah...Lord of the Rings, the most famous fantasy universe in all of literature. It shaped modern literature and is the benchmark for every book. The Lord of the Rings is a fantasy epic that is regarded as the holy grail of fantasy storytelling. The Lord of the Rings consist of three books and the three adapated movie of it all of which are masterpiece in their own right.

Premise

The Lord of the rings focus on the war of good vs evil as well as how power turns man evil. A huge focus is the ring, which is an extremely powerfull ring of immense power. It was forged by the dark lord Sauron as a way to control the other races. Wars were fough to destroy the ring but in the end, men's desire for power overcame all as they claimed the ring as their own, only to loose it.

What's so special about Lord of the Rings?

Lord of the rings is famous for many things. It's world is rich and complex with thousands of backstories. The author, Tolkien even created languages, yes, languages for his book. The worldbuilding of Lord of the Rings is unparallled and while it may not be the most unique looking from a modern point of view, its because of Lord of the Rings that its so saturated as its Lord of the Rings which created all the fantasy tropes. Even after all these work, Lord of the Rings still stand out, unmatched.
